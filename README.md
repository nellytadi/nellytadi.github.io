Block Burst — Privacy Policy
============================

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Block Burst (“the App”) is developed by Nelly Tadi. This policy explains how the App handles information.

Summary
-------

Block Burst does **not** collect, sell, or share your personal data. The App works offline and does not require an account.

Information stored on your device
---------------------------------

The App may store the following data locally on your device only:

*   **High score** — saved using Apple’s on-device storage (UserDefaults) so your best score persists between sessions.

This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Information we do not collect
-----------------------------

We do not collect:

*   Name, email address, or contact details
*   Location data
*   Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
*   Usage analytics
*   Photos, contacts, or other personal content

Third-party services
--------------------

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social networks. Audio and graphics are bundled with the App.

Children
--------

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy
----------------------

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact
-------

Questions about this policy? Contact us at [nelly.tadi@smartbox.com](mailto:nelly.tadi@smartbox.com) or via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/nellytadi/Blocks-Game/issues).
