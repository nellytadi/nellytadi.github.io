## Frequently asked questions

### How do I play?

1. Pick a shape from the tray at the bottom.
2. Drag it onto the 8×8 board (or tap a shape, then tap a cell).
3. Fill a full **row** or **column** to clear it and score points.
4. The round ends when none of your remaining shapes can fit.
5. Tap the **retry** button on the game over screen to play again.

[Full game guide →](/)

### How is my score calculated?

- **+1** per block in the shape you place  
- **+10** per row or column cleared  
- **Combo bonus:** +5 × combo level when you clear lines on consecutive placements  

### Where is my high score saved?

Your personal best is stored **only on your device** (not in the cloud). If you delete the app or install on a new device, the high score will reset.

### How do I restart a game in progress?

Tap the **reset** icon (↺) in the top-right corner of the game screen and confirm **Yes**.

### Does the app need the internet?

No. Block Burst works **fully offline**. No account is required.

### Does Block Burst collect my data?

No. We do not collect personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

### The app crashed or something looks wrong

Please email [nellytadi@gmail.com](mailto:nellytadi@gmail.com) or [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/nellytadi/Blocks-Game/issues) and include:

- Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15)
- iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- What you were doing when the problem occurred
- A screenshot if possible
