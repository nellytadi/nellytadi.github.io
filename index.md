# Block Burst

**Place blocks. Clear lines. Beat your score.**

Block Burst is a fast, satisfying block puzzle for iPhone and iPad. Drag colorful shapes onto an 8×8 board, clear full rows and columns, and see how long you can keep playing.

---

## How to play

1. **Pick a shape** from the tray at the bottom of the screen (three shapes are available at a time).
2. **Place it on the board** by dragging, or tap a shape then tap where it should go.
3. **Fill a full row or column** to clear it and earn points.
4. **Keep going** until none of your remaining shapes can fit — then the round ends.
5. **Tap retry** to start again and try to beat your personal best.

That’s it. No account, no tutorial maze — just pick up and play.

---

## Scoring

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Each block you place | +1 per block in the shape |
| Each row or column cleared | +10 per line |
| Combo bonus | +5 × combo level when you clear lines on consecutive placements |

**Tip:** Chain clears back-to-back to build your combo and push your score higher.

---

## Features

- **8×8 puzzle board** with row and column clears  
- **Drag-and-drop** placement with a live preview on the board  
- **Combo scoring** for consecutive line clears  
- **Personal high score** saved on your device  
- **Game over screen** with animated score reveal and quick retry  
- **Celebration** when you set a new personal best  
- **Sound effects** for placement, clears, and game over  
- Works in **portrait and landscape** on iPhone; **iPad** supported  

---

## Tips

- Leave space for awkward shapes — big L-pieces and long lines need room.  
- Clearing two or more lines in one move is worth more and keeps the board open.  
- Use the **reset** button (top right) if you want to abandon a round and start fresh.  
- Your **high score** appears next to the crown at the top of the screen.
